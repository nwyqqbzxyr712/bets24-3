    How a shoot-out round is built

    Each round is a direct contest between your shot and the keeper’s choice. After you set a stake, the goal is split into five scoring areas: two upper corners, two lower corners, and the centre. You pick one, the animation runs, and the result settles straight away — there are no reels, no extra bonus screens, and no side bets to manage.
